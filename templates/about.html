About Us

Who We Are

HeartGuard AI is dedicated to revolutionizing heart disease detection through artificial intelligence, striving to provide accessible, fast, and accurate heart attack predictions for early intervention. We combine expertise in cardiology, data science, and software engineering to develop cutting-edge AI algorithms that analyze diverse data sets, from medical history to real-time physiological data, to identify patterns and predict cardiac events with unprecedented accuracy. Our mission is to shift from reactive intervention to proactive prevention, empowering individuals and healthcare providers with the tools they need to take control of cardiovascular health and ultimately save lives.

Our Core Values

Clinical Excellence

We maintain rigorous medical standards, with algorithms developed in collaboration with board-certified cardiologists and validated against clinical datasets from leading research institutions.

Technological Innovation

Our engineering team continuously improves our deep learning architectures, currently utilizing transformer-based models that achieve state-of-the-art performance in temporal pattern recognition.

Patient-Centric Design

Every feature is developed with end-users in mind, ensuring accessibility for both medical professionals and patients through intuitive interfaces and clear result interpretations.

Development Milestones

2018: Foundation

Initial research partnership established with Johns Hopkins Cardiology Department to collect and analyze ECG datasets

2020: Algorithm Breakthrough

Developed proprietary CNN-LSTM hybrid architecture achieving 92.3% accuracy in silent ischemia detection

2022: Regulatory Approval

Received FDA clearance for our AI diagnostic system as Class II medical device

2023: Global Deployment

Implemented in 23 hospitals across 7 countries, processing over 150,000 ECG analyses monthly

Our Technology Stack

Advanced AI Architecture

  • Multi-modal neural networks combining ECG signals with patient metadata
  • Real-time anomaly detection using wavelet transform preprocessing
  • Explainable AI (XAI) modules for clinician interpretability
  • Continuous learning system updated with 50,000+ new ECG tracings monthly

Security Infrastructure

  • HIPAA-compliant data encryption both in transit and at rest
